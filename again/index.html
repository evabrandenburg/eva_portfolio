ABOUT ME or HOW I GOT HERE


I AM EVA

A front-end web developer based in New York City.


WHY

I got into front-end development after trying and failing to learn to code a few times. It was fun (and of course super hard) and challenging but as a life long artist I wanted to make stuff look cool not just do math. So I taught myself HTML and CSS (easier but way impressive, right?). I knew I needed to level up and finally learn to program so I took a front-end web development course at General Assembly. I improved my HTML and CSS a bunch and finally conquered my fear of coding and got into Javascript. I'm still learning (and hope to always be) and I've had a lot of fun along the way.

I do computer workshops at work but I'd love to combine both things more fully. People feel pretty awesome when they learn how to use their email but when you show someone how to alert "hello world" they totally freak! So if you're into combining those things let me know.
